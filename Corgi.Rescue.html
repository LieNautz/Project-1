About Us

Corgi Rescue is a not-for-profit organisation that rescues, rehabilitates and rehomes Corgis and mixed-breed Corgis all over Australia

We are a family run business who has been in operation since 2022, resucing and saving corgis of all breeds and finding them new loving homes

Recent Rescues

Heather

Heater is an adorable 6 month old female Pembroke, we found her lost in the streets with no tag and it had not looked like she was fed for weeks

Prince

Prince is a 2 year old male Pembroke whose owners neglected him and left him out on the streets to fend for himself. We found him close to a dumpster trying to find food and water
